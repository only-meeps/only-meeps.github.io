Consoles (Opinion)

My personal opinion on consoles right now? Handhelds are the only ones that make sense!

Forgive me if I yap for to long, but handhelds are the only consoles that make sense because of

one big thing: PC Gaming. As a lot of people know, PC's are the superior console, as you can do pretty much anything on them!

Consoles on the other hand, well, only play games (Although they do that pretty well!). Anyways, where do handhelds come into this?

Laptops are too big to carry on the go, and most games are... uh... not very great on a touchpad. So handhelds bring The Joystick!

Much worse than the mouse, but much better than the touchpad!

And most handhelds can play the same games (if not more) than a gaming laptop!

And they cost a lot less.

Anyways, after all that yapping, you probably want to know: whats your favorite console, Finn?

And I might answer a bit of a weird one: the PS vita!

I'm not gonna explain that, because that would take longer than I want to, and I also want to say my other favorite things!
